RelicBound

MYSTERIOUS FIGURE: "You cannot win this fight... not yet. The power you need lies in the past. Go back, find the four ancient relics, and rewrite your fate."


Before you can react, the figure presses a glowing symbol onto your forehead. A surge of energy courses through you. The battlefield fades, and in a blinding flash, you are flung into the depths of time.


Darkness. Silence. "Save your friends... restore peace... fulfill your destiny," echos the Mysterious Figure.
