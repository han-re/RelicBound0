Choose Your Power

Your choice will affect your journey. Choose wisely.



INVISIBILITY
Become completely invisible at will. Allowing for stealth, evasion, and surprise attacks. Players can move undetected, bypass obstacles, and avoid danger.
TELEKINESIS
Read minds and move objects using only your thoughts. Grants players control over objects with their mind and the ability to read or influence thoughts.
SUPERSTRENGTH
Supernatural strength. Allows players to lift heavy objects, break barriers, and overpower opponents with ease.
